GLYF–1: Composite Glyphs

The font for this test case has a ‘glyf’ table whose entry for U+0123 LATIN SMALL LETTER G WITH CEDILLA is a composite glyph. It has two components: one for the base glyph, and another one for the accent. A correct OpenType implementation must place the accent on top of the g.
